Pensate

In Pensate, the object is to get your game piece from the bottom of the board to the top. If you can determine the best starting position, you improve your chances of safely getting to the top of the board. There are practice and tournament modes in Pensate. There can be two players in Pensate where one player starts at the bottom and the other at the top with the goal of getting to the bottom.

Impact
Pensate is a “classic-type” game that is not a rehash of an old game like chess, but a game developed to take advantage of the capabilities of the computer, according to Softline’s May 1983 issue. This same issue had a contest for readers who could get to the top the quickest with an award of $200 worth of products from Softline’s advertisers.

Production History
Pensate was created in 1983. It was developed by John Besnard and published by Penguin Software. Penguin Software pioneered much of the field of computer graphics.
