Galactic Gladiators

In Galactic Gladiators, a tactical combat game, players simulate twenty-eighth century combat between two teams of one to ten gladiators each. Surviving gladiators gain skill with their weapons and experience points that will help them in the next battle. Unfinished and finished games can be saved for later. Each character can choose from among six options: attack immediately, move, move half the normal distance and attack, rest, load a phaser, or change weapons.

Impact
Softline’s November 1982 issue described Galactic Gladiators as a game that felt natural and would last. Galactic Gladiators was among the top rated games in Computer Gaming World’s 1983 game ratings for games from 1982 - 1983. It had an above average user rating of 6.93.

Production History
Galactic Gladiators was created in 1982. It was created by Tom Reamy. Galactic Gladiators was published by Strategic Simulations, Inc. (SSI), which was especially known for its wargames.
