Russki Duck

In Russki Duck, you are an agent for the CIA with a mission to recover stolen missile plans hidden in a “fake duck” in a shipping crate. To find the duck, you need to search through rooms in buildings picking up objects along the way needed to recover the plans. Objects must be hidden as only one can be carried at a time.

Impact
Despite a positive review in Softline’s March 1982 issue, the March 1983 issue ranked Russki Duck number 6 in a top ten list “for badness in computer games” based on reader submissions for the Dog of the Year Award.

Production History
Russki Duck was created in 1982. It was created by Eric Knopp and Alan Merrell. Russki Duck was published by Gebelli Software.
