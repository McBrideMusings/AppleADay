Softporn

Softporn Adventure (for adults only!) is a different kind of adventure game where instead of slaying dragons, players gamble and make attempts at the art of seduction. The object of the game is to find and seduce three girls (“the parser does not recognize the word woman”) with the help of money won at the casino (Softline January 1982). There are three major locations in the Softporn Adventure world: the bar, a disco, and a casino. All can be reached by taxi using the command “Hail taxi!”.

Impact
Softporn Adventure is the first commercially sold erotic game. While erotic content had circulated freely on mainframes and minicomputers previously, it had not been marketed on a commercial basis. Described as addictive by the January 1982 Softline issue, it was also criticized for its sexism: it’s a “shame that [Benton] didn’t take the time to make his program a bit classier”.

Production History
Softporn Adventure was created in 1981. It was created by Chuck Benton and Blue Sky Software for the Apple II. Softporn Adventure was published by On-line Systems (now and more commonly known as Sierra On-line).
