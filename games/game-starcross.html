Starcross

In Starcross, you are a space prospector. Your goal is to mine space for black holes in a one-man ship. You are equipped with highly sophisticated instruments to help you find the black holes. Starcross is a science fiction text adventure game rather than a fantasy adventure with puzzles that appeal to the "scientifically minded" according to Softline’s November 1982 issue.

Impact
Starcross was the first science-fiction text adventure game from Infocom (the publisher). Softline claimed that Starcross evoked more with text than many hi-res adventures did with advanced graphics. Starcross was among the top rated games in Computer Gaming World’s 1983 game ratings for games from 1982 - 1983. It had an above average user rating of 6.92.

Production History
Starcross was created in 1982. It was created by Dave Lebling. Starcross was published by Infocom. Infocom was bought by Activision in 1986 and shut down in 1989.
