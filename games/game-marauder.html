Marauder

According to the September 1982 issue of Softline, in Marauder, one of your goals is to simply run around the galaxy trashing planets. Marauder is a two-part game where you first blast away all planetary defenses. Once you have “wiped the surface of the planet clean”, you must then go through a maze of planetary wreckage and locate and destroy a power source. As the magazine states, this game should provide hours of fun.

Impact
Softline calls Marauder a game as good as any mindless arcade game and better than most. Softalk’s September 1982 issue ranked Marauder number 30 in its top thirty list.

Production History
Marauder was created in 1982. It was created by Rorke Weigandt and Eric Hammond. Marauder was published by Sierra On-line.
