Stellar 7

In Stellar 7, you use keyboard commands to steer your tank, the Raven, among enemy vehicles moving against a static background. You attempt to avoid enemy fire while maneuvering to destroy the enemy. Game play may be stopped at started at any time.

Impact
Softline’s November 1983 issue stated that the graphics and sound effects in Stellar 7 were first-rate. These features combined with its “fast-paced arcade-style excitement make Stellar 7 hard to beat in every sense” according the magazine.

Production History
Stella 7 was created in 1983. It was created by Damon Slye. Stellar 7 was published by Software Entertainment Company, a publishing company, which later became the game company Dynamix. Which was bought by Sierra On-line in 1990.
