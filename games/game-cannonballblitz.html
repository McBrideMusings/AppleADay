Cannonball Blitz

Cannonball Blitz is a Donkey Kong-type game where players must jump over cannonballs that are thrown at them by guards at the top of the ladders. The goal is to reach the flag at the top. Moving options available are up, down, left, right and jump.

Impact
Cannonball Blitz ranked 13 in the Softalk July 1983 issue’s top thirty. It was among the rated games in Computer Gaming World’s 1983 game ratings for games from 1982 - 1983. It had a below average user rating of 6.15.

Production History
Cannonball Blitz was created in 1982. It was created by Olaf Lubeck. Cannonball Blitz was published by Sierra On-line.
