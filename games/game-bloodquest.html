Impact
Blood Quest is an experimental full-paragraph parser from Brøderbund, the publisher. Softline’s November 1983 issue described it as breathtaking , strong-willed, and unforgiving. However, while you can’t get bored playing Blood Quest, “there is little you can get”.

Production History
Blood Quest was created by Elroy N. Erdling in 1983. It was published by Brøderbund Software. Brøderbund, headed by Doug Carlston - who was one a member of the “brotherhood” of game makers in the early 1980s - was purchased by The Learning Company in 1998. However, the brand can still be seen on some original Brøderbund products.
